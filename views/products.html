Loose/Bulk/Pew Envelopes

Bulk envelopes are perfect for placing in the pew, at the church entrance, or even in the bulletin. These envelopes provide a convenient way for new members to give an offering and allow members who have forgotten their regular envelopes to nonetheless get credit for their offering. These envelopes also provide visitors with a way to let you know who they are.
